Target Audience:

The target audience of the west-richland-commerce.org web site is described below:
Who: Business owners and community members
Age: 18 to 100

Personas:

  1. Persona 1:
    Name: Lindsey, Age Demographic: 20-39 years, Gender: Female, Occupation: Stay-at-home-Mom.
  2. Persona 2:
    Name: Robin, Age Demographic: 60-79, Gender: Female, Occupation: Business Owner of Walmart store.

Scenarios:

  1. What are these people looking for in a commerce website?
  2. How do we get people to join the commerce?
  3. Do we have enough information on the local economy to be advocated for this area?
  4. How do you establish Commerce?
  5. Why do we need a City of Commerce in the first place?
