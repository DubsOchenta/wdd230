PARC: Proximity

harborfreight.com

The reason that I feel this website shows the use of Proximity is because of how the search bar, large coupon deals link, and the logo for the website are all close to eachother but are varying size and distance from eachother.

Proximity Definition: The relationship between items on a page and site is critical for conveying a message and purpose to the user. Proximity deals with the placement of items and content on a page to establish the relative strength of a relationship between items.

PARC: Contrast

nintendo.com

Why I feel that this is a good example of Contrast is how the different elements of the site are layered. It has a hovering nav bar and a static header. both of these combine to create good contrast.

Contrast Definition: Contrast is designing a distinct difference between foreground and background colors in order to enhance readability. Contrast does not only deal with colors but also blends into visual hierarchy of font sizes, image placement, and content.

White Space and Clean Design

google.com

The reason that I feel this is a good example for White Space is because it uses the white space efficiently to narrow you in on the most important part, the search bar and the Google logo.

White Space Definition- One of the biggest violations in beginning web design is the lack of proper white space, padding, and use of the space between elements of a design. This violation is seen on the page layout as a whole and with simple elements like the input elements on a form where there are natural and purposeful borders. We can enhance the user's experience with readability and increase comprehension of intent by using white space and appropriate padding between content and borders.
